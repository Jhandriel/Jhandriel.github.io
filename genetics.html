Genetics Experiment

The Initial

In New Media and tech class, we were tasked with collecting pieces of our DNA in order to match them and find any matches between students at ESUMS.DNA collection required us to use materials such as

  1. toothpick
  2. micro pipetter
  3. mini pcr
. In our first step we used the micro pipetter to extract primer from a tube to then transport it into our test tube. After doing so we grabbed a toothpick and swabbed the inside of our check to collect some DNA cells. After our cells were collected, we added another liquid into our test tube using the pipetter before inserting our Toothpick full of DNA into the tube. After mixing our DNA in the tube for a couple seconds, we moved our tubes over to the Mini PCR to began the DNA extraction process. However, the process didn't finish there, we moved onto the gel electrophoreses machine which showed us how quick how cells travel through a gel. During this process, we were able to determine how many base pairs our DNA contained because of the distance travelled in the gel, with longer DNA travelling less, and smaller DNA traveling more. In the End.

MINI PCR

This process of continuous cooling and heating took around 1 hour. At the end of this cycle, 1 billion copies of DNA were made! How interesting!

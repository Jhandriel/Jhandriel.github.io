Black Box Investigation

CER Response

Claim

In New media and tech, we were challenged with figuring out what is in the mystery black box at ESUMS . The Black Box is a box with a function that causes more, less, or no water at all to come out when water is added, so we need to figure out this specific function. I believe inside of the mystery box, there are 2-3 tubes, a bucket that tips into another tube at a certain amount of water, and maybe but not certainly, a waterwheel.

Evidence

Based on initial observations, I saw that every time we put water in, a certain amount wasn’t expected to come out, and sometimes even no water would come out which could be due to the water bucket not tipping. For example, after running 6 trials, we ran 2 tests in which 0 mL of water was our output and on trial 6 we had an input of 1000 and an output of 1710 mL. Additionally, I built my model based on my thoughts and it supported my claim because our data proves that the black box has weight controlled water dropper. Some limitations may be that our box is made of cardboard so it may not be as stable as expected which can influence results. Some strengths can be that we have brand new materials and technology which can lead to a more accurate measure and depiction of what's going on inside our box despite the limitations.

Reasoning

This backs up my claim because due to my strengths, my black box is a credible resource and is expected to match accurate results of the original black box.
